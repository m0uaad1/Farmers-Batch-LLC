Our Working Formula (10 Points)

  1. Plan: Walk the field and align on targets and timing.
  2. Crew Size: Right number of workers per task and acre.
  3. Safety: Hydration, breaks, PPE, and lift limits.
  4. Lead: One onsite lead for coordination and QC.
  5. Tools: We bring basics; we integrate with your equipment.
  6. Pacing: Clear row assignments and progress checks.
  7. Quality: Random checks and quick retraining if needed.
  8. Reporting: End-of-day summary—acres, boxes, issues.
  9. Flex: Add/trim headcount as conditions change.
  10. Aftercare: Site tidy, waste removed, next-day readiness.
